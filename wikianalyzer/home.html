WIKI:ANALYZER offers a new way to digest Wikipedia articles. The readers are encouraged to compare the changing language of different versions of the same article. The readers will be able to see the shift (or consistency) in public’s interest, opinion, and attitude toward the subject over time. The texts are organized in reverse-chronological order so the most recent version appears first. To further understand the components of WIKI:ANALYZER, please look at the keys provided below.
