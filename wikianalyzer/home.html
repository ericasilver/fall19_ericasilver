WIKI:ANALYZER offers a new way to digest Wikipedia articles. Instead of displaying the most recent version, each revisions are treated as a separate text. By comparing the difference between each version, which are taken a year apart from the date the first version was published, the readers will be able to see the change in public perception of the subject matter. The texts are organized in reverse-chronological order, so the most relevant version appears first.
